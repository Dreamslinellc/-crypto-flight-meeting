What is the Crypto Flight Meeting?

An exclusive in-flight event where blockchain founders, investors, and innovators come together to pitch, connect, and accelerate deals. While cruising above the clouds, you'll get the chance to:

  • Pitch your project during “air pitch” sessions
  • Network with selected top-tier crypto leaders
  • Join mentorship and funding breakout sessions onboard

Why in the air?

The airborne setting removes distractions, fosters trust, and fast-tracks decisions. It's the ideal environment to validate ideas, close deals, and expand your network with Web3 power players.
